# QRDROP 📱

🔗 **Live:** [#](#)

Ever had a long URL on your PC that you just wanted to open on your phone without typing the whole thing out? Yeah, that's exactly why this exists.

---

## What does it do?

You paste a link. It gives you a QR code. You scan it with your phone. That's literally it.

---

## How to use it

1. Go to [#](#)
2. Paste any link
3. Hit **Generate**
4. Scan with your phone camera
5. Done 🎉

---

## Little extras

- You can make the QR bigger or smaller
- Change the colors if you're feeling fancy
- Download it as an image if you want to save or share it
- Works on your phone too if you ever need it

---

## That's it

No setup. No internet needed. No accounts. Just open the file and go.
